I had a blast working with the NFB and director Randall Okita on this project. I got use some Unity features for the first time like Timeline and the Universal Render Pipeline. I learned to capture and integrate motion capture animation data using an XSens suite (I even jumped in the suit for a few sessions).

I programmed a lot of interactions and effects for this game, including a paint brush reveal effect using a compute shader and having hundreds of plants grow in a field where you throw seeds. I am also very proud of the blast and paper desolve effect. It was important to get the right tone for this moment and I worked very closely with Randall to get it right.
